Who We Are

Equinox Health Supplies is a trusted medical infrastructure and equipment provider based in Nairobi, Kenya. We specialize in designing, equipping, and setting up high-standard medical environments tailored to your specific healthcare needs.

From mobile emergency response units to static clinical and diagnostic facilities, we deliver turnkey solutions built for efficiency, compliance, and patient care across all 47 counties in Kenya.

Our Core Services

Ambulance Conversions & Setups

Transforming vehicles into fully equipped, life-saving mobile intensive care units. Complete with medical-grade equipment, oxygen systems, and patient transport solutions.

Clinic Setups

Designing and outfitting modern medical clinics from the ground up. Ensuring optimal patient flow, regulatory compliance, and fully equipped consultation rooms.

Laboratory Setups

Installing state-of-the-art diagnostic environments equipped with precise layout planning and essential medical technology for accurate testing and analysis.
